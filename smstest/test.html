    Product Description
    Robust Processor

    Sporting the Intel Athlon Silver 3050U processor with up to 256 GB HDD storage, the Dell Inspiron 15 3525 Laptop facilitates highly responsive and silent performance. Moreover, thanks to its expansive keyboard with a numeric keypad, large keycaps, and a spacious touchpad, this laptop lets you easily search for your desired content. Besides, this laptop's lift hinge allows you to elevate it to an ergonomic position, enabling you to type comfortably. And, this laptop significantly minimises the amount of blue light emitted with the help of Dell ComfortView Low Blue Light (LBL) technology, allowing you to work or play for extended periods of time without straining your eyes. Additionally, courtesy of its large HD display of up to 39.62 cm (15.6) with up to three-side narrow borders, this laptop delivers an impactful visual experience.

    Modern Design

    Owing to its inbuilt HD webcam, this laptop allows for high-quality video calling, enabling you to efficiently interact with friends and family. Furthermore, designed with small rubber feet and bumpers on the hinge, this laptop is protected from skidding even when placed on hard surfaces while ensuring additional stability, making it suitable to use on a regular basis.

    Sustainable Design

    Built with post-consumer recycled plastics, this laptop is environment-friendly and sustainable. And, to ensure the utmost safety, the painted portions of this laptop employ low Volatile Organic Compounds (VOC) waterborne paint.

    Rapid Charging

    Integrated with ExpressCharge, this laptop can charge its battery to up to 80% in 60 minutes. So, you wont have to sit next to a power outlet frequently when using this laptop.
